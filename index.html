Crop Details

A "crop" is any plant tha is cultivated and harvested for food. Crops are classified as food crops, feed crops, fiber crops, oil crops, ornamental crops, and industrial crops based on their purpose. such as vegetables, are harvested for human consumption.

Farming Products

Food crops, such as fruits and vegetables, are harvested for human consumption. Grains, such as corn, wheat, and rice, fibers, fuels, and raw materials are the world's most popular food crops. Food crops were the first to be harvested through agriculture.
